**Bold Two-Column** is for candidates who want presence: a dark sidebar, badge-style section
headings, and a confident accent color. It is a fit for marketing, sales, and growth roles
where a memorable PDF helps you stand out.

Build it in the editor, set an accent color that suits you, and download a selectable-text PDF.
For postings that run strict automated screening, switch to a single-column template — your
content carries over when you change templates.
