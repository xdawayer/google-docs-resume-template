**Functional** leads with a boxed Core Competencies grid, then follows with a condensed,
dated work history. It is built for career changers and skills-forward candidates who want
recruiters to see their transferable strengths first.

Fill it in directly in the builder: group your competencies, keep the work history brief but
dated, and download a selectable-text PDF. If a role wants a traditional timeline, switch to
a chronological template and your content carries over.
