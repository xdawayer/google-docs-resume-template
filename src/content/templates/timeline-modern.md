**Timeline** marks each role with a vertical accent rail and dots, giving project, product,
and operations managers a clear sense of career progression. Work experience and
projects ride the rail; everything else stays clean and single-column.

Build it in the editor, set an accent that suits you, and download a selectable-text PDF.
For postings that run strict automated screening, switch to a single-column template —
your content carries over when you change templates.
