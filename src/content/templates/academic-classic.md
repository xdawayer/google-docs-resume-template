**Academic** is a restrained serif resume for teaching, research, and academic roles.
Left-aligned small-caps headings and hairline rules give it a scholarly, print-classic feel
without sacrificing a clean single-column structure.

Fill it in directly in the builder, reorder sections so education and certifications lead if
that suits your field, and download a selectable-text PDF that an applicant tracking
system reads top to bottom.
