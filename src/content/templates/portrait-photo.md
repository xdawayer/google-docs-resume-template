**Portrait** pairs a circular headshot with your name in a softly tinted header band,
over a clean single-column body. It is built for client-facing and international
applications — real estate, hospitality, sales, and hospitality-adjacent roles — where a
personal photo is expected.

Fill it in directly in the builder: drop in a photo (or leave it blank for tidy initials),
recolor the accent, and download a selectable-text PDF. For applications that run strict
automated screening, switch to a photo-free template and your content moves with you.
