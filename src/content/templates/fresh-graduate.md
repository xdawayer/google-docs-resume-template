**Fresh Graduate** is built for a first real resume: it leads with your objective, education,
and projects, so coursework, internships, and campus work do the talking when your job history
is still short.

Fill it in the builder, reorder sections so your strongest material comes first, and download a
selectable-text PDF. If a posting screens through strict ATS software, the single-column Modern
ATS or the Student Internship Google Doc are safer companions.
