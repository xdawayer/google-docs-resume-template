**Corporate** is a conservative, formal resume for finance, accounting, and corporate
roles. The name sits in a navy-ruled letterhead frame, with navy headings and bold,
right-aligned dates for a polished, results-forward look.

Fill it in directly in the builder, lead with quantified results and certifications such as
CPA or CFA, and download a selectable-text PDF. The single-column layout stays
ATS-readable end to end.
