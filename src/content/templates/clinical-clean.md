**Clinical** is a calm, trustworthy resume for nursing, allied health, and caregiving
roles. Teal accent bars mark each section, and a licenses-and-certifications block keeps
your RN license, BLS, and ACLS easy to find.

Fill it in directly in the builder, list your clinical experience and credentials, and
download a selectable-text PDF. The single-column layout stays readable to the
application systems hospitals use.
