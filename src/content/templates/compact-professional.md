**Compact** is built to fit years of experience on a single page: a thin accent top bar, tight
but readable spacing, condensed entries, and a two-column skills grid. It suits senior and
experienced candidates who have a lot to say.

Fill it in directly in the builder, lead each role with quantified results, and download a
clean, ATS-readable PDF. The density lives in the spacing, so the layout stays single-column
and easy for software to parse.
