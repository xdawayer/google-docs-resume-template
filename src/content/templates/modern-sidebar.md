**Modern Sidebar** puts your contact details and skills in a colored left rail and gives your
experience room to breathe on the right. It is a strong fit for engineers, product, and
project managers presenting a PDF to a recruiter or hiring manager.

Build it in the editor, choose an accent color, and download a selectable-text PDF. If your
application runs through strict automated screening first, switch to a single-column template —
the builder keeps your content when you change templates.
