**Header Band** leads with a full-width color banner carrying your name and contact
details, then drops into a clean single-column body. It suits sales, account management,
and business roles where a confident first impression helps.

Build it in the editor, set an accent that fits you, and download a selectable-text PDF.
The body stays single-column and scanner-first, so it reads cleanly to an applicant
tracking system even with the bold header.
