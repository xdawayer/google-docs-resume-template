**Minimalist** is a study in restraint: a large, light-weight name, a single short accent
stroke, hairline section rules, and generous whitespace. Nothing competes with your
words. It suits any role and any experience level.

Fill it in directly in the builder, adjust the accent or spacing if you like, and download a
clean, selectable-text PDF that an applicant tracking system reads top to bottom.
