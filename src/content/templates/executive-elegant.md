**Executive Elegant** gives senior candidates a composed, single-column layout: a centered
serif name, restrained spacing, and hairline rules that read as seniority without shouting.
It keeps to one page so a board member or hiring partner can scan your track record fast.

Open it in the builder, drop in your roles and measurable outcomes, and download a
selectable-text PDF. Because it is a single column with standard headings, the elegance is
visual only — applicant tracking systems still read every line in order.
