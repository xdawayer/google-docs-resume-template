**Technical** is a compact, single-column resume for software engineers, data, and
platform roles. A left-aligned header, accent section headings, and a skills row of
clean tags keep it scannable for both a recruiter and their software.

Fill it in directly in the builder: list your stack, quantify your impact, and download a
selectable-text PDF. Switch the accent color or font without breaking the single-column
structure that keeps it ATS-friendly.
