**Modern ATS** is the safest choice when your resume has to clear an applicant tracking
system before a human ever sees it. A single column, plain section headings, and no
tables or text boxes mean the parser reads your experience in the exact order you wrote it.

Fill it in directly in the builder: type your details, reorder sections to match the role,
and download a clean PDF with selectable text. Switch the font, size, and accent color
without breaking the single-column structure that keeps it ATS-friendly.
